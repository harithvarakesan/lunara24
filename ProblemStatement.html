Frequently Asked Questions

What's Hack-A-Thon? ▶

Hack-A-Thon is an offline 24-hour long hackathon where you code in a competitive fashion. You can participate alone or in a team.

What's the minimum and maximum team size? ▶

A team could comprise 2 to 4 members.

How do I submit my project? ▶

Submit your project in the github repository.

What does the judging criteria look like? ▶

Evaluation criteria includes presentation, documentation, and originality.
